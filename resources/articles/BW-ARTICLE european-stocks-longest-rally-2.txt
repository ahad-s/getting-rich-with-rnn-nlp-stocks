European stocks extended their gains into a fourth day ahead of the European Central Bank’s meeting today, with all eyes on President Mario Draghi for cues on further stimulus. France’s CAC 40 Index and Italy’s FTSE MIB Index extended their gains in bull-market territory, while the Stoxx Europe 600 Index added 0.2 percent at 8:23 a.m. in London.
