DUBLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Research and Markets has announced the addition of the "Global Smart TV and Streaming Media Devices Markets: Forecast to 2021" report to their offering.
This study provides an in-depth analysis of the global streaming media devices market and the smart TV market. The thin client paradigm, where broadcast, pay TV, and on-demand content is delivered directly via the Internet to consumers, continues to grow.
Streaming media devices, smart TVs, and to some extent game consoles and tablets, are at the forefront of this transition. The base year used in the research is 2016, and unit shipment forecasts as well as discussion of major market drivers and restraints are provided for the years 2017 through 2021.
