Note to Editors:
MV Index Solutions (MVIS) develops, monitors and licenses the MVIS Indices, a selection of focused, investable and diversified benchmark indices which are especially designed to underlie financial products. MVIS Indices cover several asset classes, including hard assets and international equity markets as well as fixed income markets. Approximately USD 12.9 billion in assets under management are currently invested in financial products based on MVIS Indices. MVIS is a VanEck company.
