DUBLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Research and Markets has announced the addition of the "World: Chandeliers - Market Report - Analysis and Forecast to 2020" report to their offering.
The report provides an in-depth analysis of the global chandelier market. It presents the latest data of the market size and volume, domestic production, exports and imports, price dynamics and turnover in the industry. The report shows the sales data, allowing you to identify the key drivers and restraints. You can find here a strategic analysis of key factors influencing the market. Forecasts illustrate how the market will be transformed in the medium term. Profiles of the leading companies and brands are also included.
From 2007 to 2015, global exports of chandeliers showed a robust growth trend, with the exception of a minor decline in 2009. Exports amounted to 16,852 million USD in 2015. There was an annual increase of +9.2% throughout the analyzed period.
According to estimates, China continued to lead the way in global exports of chandeliers, claiming an even larger share towards the end of the period under review. In 2015, China's exports of chandeliers totaled 8,673 million USD, which accounted for a 51% share in terms of global exports. Mexico, Germany, Italy, and USA were the other key global suppliers of chandeliers in 2015, with a 22% combined share of global exports.
