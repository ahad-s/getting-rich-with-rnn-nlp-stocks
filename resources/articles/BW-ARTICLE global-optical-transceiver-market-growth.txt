DUBLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Research and Markets has announced the addition of the "Global Optical Transceiver Market 2016-2020" report to their offering.
The report forecasts the global optical transceiver market to grow at a CAGR of 16.3% during the period 2016-2020.
Optical transceivers are used in several applications including commercial, industrial, and laboratory. Optical transceivers function effectively when used in conjunction with fiber optic amplifiers, fiber optic ring lasers, fiber optic communication systems, and high-speed/dense wavelength division multiplexing (DWDM) and coherent fiber optic links, laboratory R&D, sensors, gyro-systems, and test/instrumentation measurement quality assurance applications in the automation of manufacturing processes.
The report covers the present scenario and the growth prospects of the global optical transceiver market for 2016-2020. To calculate the market size, the report considers the revenue generated from the shipment of optical transceivers worldwide across the various segments such as data centers, telecommunications, and other (enterprise, defense, and media and entertainment).
According to the report, rising number of FTTx connections to drive growth in this market. FTTx stands for "fiber to the x," help reduce the installation price for high bandwidth services to the home. These architectures include FTTC or fiber to the curb (also called FTTN or fiber to the node), FTTH or fiber to the home, and FTTP or fiber to the premises, where premises may indicate homes, small businesses, or apartments.
