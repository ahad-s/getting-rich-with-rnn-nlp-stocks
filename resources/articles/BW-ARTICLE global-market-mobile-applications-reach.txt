DUBLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Research and Markets has announced the addition of the "Mobile Technologies: Global Markets" report to their offering.
The global market for mobile applications was valued at nearly $10.9 billion in 2015. This market is expected to increase from $12.2 billion in 2016 to nearly $24.0 billion in 2021 at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 14.4% for 2016-2021.
This report provides an overview of the global market for mobile technologies. It includes analyses of global market trends, with data from 2015, 2016, and projections of compound annual growth rates (CAGRs) through 2021. An exploration of the total available market for mobile applications and segmentation by category, as well as discussion of key software development platforms are also included.
The report provides examination of the marketplace for mobile applications by pricing strategies and business models, and by gaming, multimedia, and personal use. It segments mobile applications by the most popular operating systems in the market: Android, iOS and Windows. For each segment, the report provides estimated total market, number of applications, and average revenue per application.
