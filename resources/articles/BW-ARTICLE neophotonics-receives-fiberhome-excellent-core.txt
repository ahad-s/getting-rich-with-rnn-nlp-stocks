SHENZHEN, China & SAN JOSE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
NeoPhotonics, a leading designer and manufacturer of advanced hybrid photonic integrated optoelectronic modules and subsystems for bandwidth-intensive, high-speed communications networks, today announced that it has received the prestigious Excellent Core Partner Award for 2016 from FiberHome Telecommunications Technologies, one of the world's leading providers of telecommunications network solutions. The Excellent Core Partner Award is the highest honor given to a supplier by FiberHome, and was presented at a ceremony at FiberHome’s headquarters in the “Optics Valley” in Wuhan, China, near FiberHome’s headquarters. NeoPhotonics was honored for the second year in a row for its contributions as a supplier of innovative optical technology and products for high-speed communications networks.
"We strive to provide the highest value to our customers through high-performance and high-quality products and to leverage our supply chain and manufacturing capacity to support their business. We are grateful to receive this prestigious honor from FiberHome for the second year in a row," said Tim Jenks, Chairman and CEO of NeoPhotonics. "We have served FiberHome with high speed products for several years, and we have expanded our technology, quality and manufacturing capabilities to support FiberHome’s rapid growth during this period."
FiberHome Telecommunications Technologies is part of the FiberHome Technologies Group, a leading product manufacturer and solution provider in the field of information technology and telecommunications, with approximately five billion U.S. dollars in annual revenue. As the major high-tech enterprise directly affiliated to the State-owned Assets Supervision and Administration Commission of the State Council, FiberHome Technologies is the core enterprise located in Wuhan Optics Valley of China.
About NeoPhotonics
NeoPhotonics is a leading designer and manufacturer of advanced hybrid photonic integrated optoelectronic modules and subsystems for bandwidth-intensive, high-speed communications networks. The Company’s products enable cost-effective, high-speed data transmission and efficient allocation of bandwidth over communications networks. NeoPhotonics maintains headquarters in San Jose, California and ISO 9001:2000 certified engineering and manufacturing facilities in Silicon Valley (USA), Japan and China.
