BENSALEM, Pa.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Law Offices of Howard G. Smith announces an investigation on behalf of investors of Fred’s, Inc. (“Fred’s” or the “Company”) (NASDAQ: FRED) investors concerning the Company and its officers’ potential violation of federal securities laws.
Fred’s, together with its subsidiaries, sells general merchandise through its retail discount stores and full service pharmacies.
On December 8, 2016, Fred’s reported a net loss of $38.4 million for the third quarter of 2016, compared to a net income of $1.4 million for the same period last year. Then, on the Company’s earnings call, the Company stated that it would not take analysts’ questions due to a “pending transaction.”
