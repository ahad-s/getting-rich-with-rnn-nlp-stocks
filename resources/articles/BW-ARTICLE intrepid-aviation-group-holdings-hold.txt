STAMFORD, Conn.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Intrepid Aviation Group Holdings Inc. (the “Company”) announced today that it will hold its 3rd Quarter 2016 Investor Call on December 7, 2016 at 10:00AM Eastern. The call is not open to the public and is only available for all holders of the Company’s 6.875% Senior Notes due 2019 and the 8.25% Senior Notes due in 2017. The dial-in details for the call be accessed on the Company’s password protected website (HTTPS://intrepidaviation.net).
