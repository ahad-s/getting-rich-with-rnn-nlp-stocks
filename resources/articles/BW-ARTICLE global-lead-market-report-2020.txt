DUBLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Research and Markets has announced the addition of the "World: Lead - Market Report - Analysis and Forecast to 2020" report to their offering.
This report has been designed to provide a detailed analysis of the global lead market. It covers the most recent data sets of quantitative medium-term projections, as well as developments in production, trade, consumption and prices. The report also includes a comparative analysis of the leading consuming countries, revealing opportunities opened for producers and exporters across the globe. The forecast outlines market prospects to 2020.
The global lead trade amounted to 5581 million USD in 2015, fluctuating consistently over the period under review. Exports were on a decline in 2008 and 2009, recovering briefly in 2010 and 2011. 2012 saw another dip, until exports reversed the trend in 2013 and 2014, showing very modest growth. Finally, exports ended 2015 with a 9% decline from the value of 2014. Overall, there was an annual decrease of -0.3% from 2007 to 2015.
