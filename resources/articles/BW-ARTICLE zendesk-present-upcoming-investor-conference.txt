SAN FRANCISCO--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Zendesk, Inc. (ZEN) today announced that Elena Gomez, Chief Financial Officer, and Adrian McDermott, President of Products, will present at the Evercore ISI Cloud Transformation Day at the Omni San Francisco Hotel in San Francisco, California.
Zendesk’s presentation is scheduled for Tuesday, December 6, 2016 at 4:00 p.m., Pacific Time. A live webcast and an archived version will be available for six months. Both will be accessible by visiting Zendesk’s investor website at investor.zendesk.com.
About Zendesk:
Zendesk builds software for better customer relationships. It empowers organizations to improve customer engagement and better understand their customers. More than 87,000 paid customer accounts in over 150 countries and territories use Zendesk products. Based in San Francisco, Zendesk has operations in the United States, Europe, Asia, Australia, and South America. Learn more at www.zendesk.com.
