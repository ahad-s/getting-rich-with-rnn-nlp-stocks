About Nonda
Founded in Palo Alto, CA in 2015, Nonda leads the market in offering connected car devices driven by innovation, that are simple and accessible to everyone. Nonda believes that every car can be a connected car and has successfully crowdfunded three products on Indiegogo (ZUS Smart Car Charger, ZUS Kevlar Cable) and Kickstarter (Hub+ for the Apple MacBook 12). In 2015 Nonda won SVIEF’s (Silicon Valley Innovation & Entrepreneurship Forum) "The fastest growing startup" and "The best 10 smart hardware” awards, and in 2016, two of Nonda's products won prestigious iF & Red dot design awards.
