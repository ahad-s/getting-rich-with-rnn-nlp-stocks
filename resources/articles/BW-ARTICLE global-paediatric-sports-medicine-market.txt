Research and Markets has announced the addition of the "Global Paediatric Sports Medicine Market Analysis & Trends - Product, Distribution Channel, Application - Forecast to 2025" report to their offering.
Some of the prominent trends that the market is witnessing include growing occurrence of health affiliated issues among individuals, rising prevalence of sports related injuries in young athletes, growth opportunities/investment opportunities and recent technological developments of paediatric sports medicine.
Based on product type, the market is categorized into oral and topical. By oral, the market is further divided into syrups and tablets. By topical, the market is further segmented into sprays, creams and gels.
Depending on the distribution channel, the market is segmented into sports academies, e-commerce, hospitals, private clinics and pharmacies and drug stores. By application, the market is further segregated into knee injuries, back & spine injuries, foot & ankle injuries, hip & groin injuries, elbow & wrist injuries, shoulder injuries and other injuries.
The study focuses on market trends, leading players, supply chain trends, technological innovations, key developments, and future strategies. With comprehensive market assessment across the major geographies such as North America, Europe, Asia Pacific, Middle East, Latin America and Rest of the world the report is a valuable asset for the existing players, new entrants and the future investors.
