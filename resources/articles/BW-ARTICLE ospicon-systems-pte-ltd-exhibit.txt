About Ospicon Systems Pte Ltd
Ospicon mission is to improve sleep wellness and safety for infant and adult care through the invention, improvement, and application of user-friendly and intuitive technologies. Ospicon started 2014 with offices in Singapore, Hong Kong and Shenzhen. Employing more than 30 personnel with collective experience of more than 500 man-years. Core competencies in Bluetooth Technology and Optics Fiber sensing. Ospicon has businesses in Connected Logistic, US Correction Institution, Infant breath/sleep monitor and Adult Sleep Wellness segments. Hold patents in fiber optics technology and patent pending in BLE application for transponder RFID.
