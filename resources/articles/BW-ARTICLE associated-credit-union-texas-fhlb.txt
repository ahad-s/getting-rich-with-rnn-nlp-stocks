DEER PARK, Texas--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
The Federal Home Loan Bank of Dallas (FHLB Dallas) and the Associated Credit Union of Texas, an FHLB Dallas member institution, will present a $16,000 Partnership Grant Program (PGP) award to the Keystone Community Development Corporation (Keystone CDC). The event will take place at 10:30 a.m., Monday, December 12, 2016, at the Keystone CDC office, 309 West X Street, Deer Park, Texas, 77536.
The media is encouraged to attend.
Keystone CDC is a 501(c)3 nonprofit that provides homebuyer education classes, down-payment assistance grants, financial empowerment counseling, and other resources. The PGP grant from Associated Credit Union of Texas and FHLB Dallas will be used to support the nonprofitâ€™s homebuyer education classes.
Through the PGP, member institutions contribute $500 up to $4,000 to a community-based organization (CBO). FHLB Dallas matches the contributions at a 3:1 ratio, which provide up to $12,000 in grant money to a CBO. In 2016, FHLB Dallas awarded $225,000 in PGP funds to assist 23 CBOs. Combined with the $96,200 from member institutions, a total of $321,200 was awarded this year. For more information, visit fhlb.com.
