DUBLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Research and Markets has announced the addition of the "Global Aircraft Carriers Market 2016-2020" report to their offering.
The report forecasts the global aircraft carriers market to grow at a CAGR of 2.11% during the period 2016-2020.
Aircraft carriers are warships that serve as aquatic airbases, equipped with a full-length flight deck and facilities for carriage, deployment, and recovery of aircraft. It is the ship that projects air power globally, without depending on the local airbases. These vessels are capable of open ocean operations even in rough seas. These ships are equipped with armor and can fight enemies across the water.
The report covers the present scenario and the growth prospects of the global aircraft carriers market for 2016-2020. To calculate the market size, the report considers the revenue generated from the sales of conventional-powered aircraft carriers and nuclear-powered aircraft carriers.
The emergence of advanced naval technologies and features has changed the defense equipment development process. There have been rapid advancements with respect to the range, lethality, accuracy, preventive aids, stealth characteristics, and mobility of aircraft carriers. Most of the next-generation aircraft carriers that are being developed are being incorporated with new and cutting-edge technologies.
