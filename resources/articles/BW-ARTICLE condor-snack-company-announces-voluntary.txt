DENVER--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
As part of the voluntary recall of sweet cream buttermilk powder made by Valley Milk Products LLC, an ingredient in All American Seasoning, Inc.’s sour cream and onion seasoning, Condor Snack Company is voluntarily recalling certain lots of 2.5, 6 and 11 ounce Kroger® Brand Sour Cream & Onion Potato Chips because they have the potential to be contaminated with Salmonella.
This Smart News Release features multimedia. View the full release here: http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20161209005802/en/ 
Salmonella is a bacteria that can cause diarrhea, fever, and abdominal cramps and most individuals recover without treatment. In some cases, diarrhea may be so severe that the patient needs to be hospitalized. The elderly, infants, and those with impaired immune systems are more likely to have a severe illness.
NO ILLNESSES ASSOCIATED WITH THIS RECALL HAVE BEEN REPORTED.
The product being voluntarily recalled is noted below:
NO OTHER CONDOR SNACK COMPANY PRODUCTS ARE BEING RECALLED.
Please see image of Package Front and Best If Used By Date.
Consumers are urged NOT to eat this product. Consumers who purchased this product may return it to the Kroger or City Market store where it was purchased for a full refund or exchange, or they may simply discard the product.
For further information please contact Condor Snack Company, (303) 333-6075, Monday through Friday 8:30 am to 5:00 pm MST.
