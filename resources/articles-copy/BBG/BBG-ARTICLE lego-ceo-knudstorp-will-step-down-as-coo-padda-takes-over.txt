Lego A/S Chief Executive Officer Jorgen Vig Knudstorp, who pulled the Danish company out of a crisis to make it the world’s most profitable toymaker, will step down from his position to head a new branding division within the group.Chief Operations Officer Bali Padda will take over the CEO job when Knudstorp leaves at the end of the year, the Billund, Denmark-based company said Tuesday in a website statement.Lego is a closely held company controlled by the billionaire Kjeld Kirk Kristiansen.
