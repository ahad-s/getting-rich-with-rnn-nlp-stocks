NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
EasyVista Inc., a leading provider of service management for IT organizations, today announced that Gary Mellott has joined EasyVista as Vice President of Sales - Western Region. His appointment follows an $8.4M investment in EasyVista from investors in Europe and the United States marked for product development and expanding market opportunities in North America where the company recently announced record earnings and growth.
This Smart News Release features multimedia. View the full release here: http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20161201005120/en/ 
“Gary’s extensive industry experience matched with EasyVista’s vision for the future of service management is a perfect fit,” said Kevin Coppins, EasyVista General Manager, Americas. “His appointment is an important addition to our North American sales team as we continue to expand our client base, building on the market momentum we’ve seen for our mobile-first ITSM solutions.”
Mellott is an accomplished senior sales executive who has held strategic roles in business and channel development of IT Service Management (ITSM) IT Service Support Management (ITSSM) and Customer Relationship Management (CRM) applications. Gary’s 16 years in ITSM include Managing Director of Emerging Markets and Director of Business Development at Cherwell Software, and VP of Worldwide Sales at FrontRange Solutions, now HEAT Software.
“I am excited to join EasyVista, a company that has extraordinary vision and capabilities to address today’s most urgent market needs for ITSM 2.0,” said Mellott. “The service desk is indeed shaping up to be the on-ramp to the digital workplace where mobility is crucial. EasyVista’s visionary product capabilities and their demonstrated commitment to customer success match today’s market perfectly, it’s a great time to be a part of it.”
About EasyVista
EasyVista is reinventing service management for the mobile user — making it easy to deliver and easy to use. EasyVista is a service management platform that automates and personalizes service delivery for employees and other end users — without a single line of code. The only solution on the market that is purpose-built as a mobile-first experience, EasyVista helps 1,000+ enterprises around the world radically improve the service experience, dramatically simplify and accelerate service creation and management and reduce and control the total cost of service delivery. With more than 20 years in service management, EasyVista serves companies across a variety of industries, including financial services, healthcare, higher education, technology, public sector, retail, manufacturing and more. Headquartered in Paris and New York, EasyVista is traded on the French stock exchange as ALEZV:EN.
