CHERRY HILL, N.J.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
AmeriQuest Business Services, a leading provider of financial process automation, procurement, and asset management solutions, today announced that Sean Bliss has been promoted to Vice President of Sales for Indirect Spend Solutions. Since joining AmeriQuest in June, 2016, Sean has been the Director of Product and Services.
In this new role, Sean will lead our Indirect Spend Division expanding AmeriQuest’s program offerings that provide customers with additional saving opportunities in their indirect spend categories.
“Indirect spend is an increasingly important and crucial area of focus for businesses,” said Bill McCouch, Senior Vice President of Procurement Services. “I’m excited that Sean’s expertise will help us elevate and accelerate the value in managing indirect spend, for all of our customers.”
Prior to joining AmeriQuest, Sean spent two years with The Hertz Corporation and five years with IBM’s Global Business Services practice as a Senior Managing Consultant, specializing in the strategic sourcing of indirect spend. As a strategic sourcing specialist, Sean brings over 15 years of procurement transformation experience to his role.
He received his Master of Business Administration degree from the University of Notre Dame.
About AmeriQuest Business Services
AmeriQuest is a technology-enabled provider of financial process automation, procurement, and asset management solutions. AmeriQuest enables companies to simplify and streamline their complex business processes through a combination of technology, expertise, and process efficiencies, and gives them the operational leverage they need to grow their business.
