DUBLIN--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Research and Markets has announced the addition of the "Kenya ICT Outlook: 2017" report to their offering.
The study aims to analyze and forecast the Kenya ICT (Information and Communication Technologies) market outlook for 2017.
Market Measurements are analyzed for the year of 2016 and 2017- Kenyan ICT market snapshot; fixed-line, mobile, internet/data, broadband, mobile money penetration; and 2016-2017 growth rate sector-wise. An extensive list of start-ups to watch out for in 2016 is discussed in detail followed by disruptors in the ICT industry.
Strategies, growth analysis, competitive landscape, and future focus are discussed for IT service providers (Seven Seas Technologies, IBM, Tech Mahindra, Oracle, etc.), and telecommunications providers (Safaricom, Liquid Telecom, Airtel, Orange, etc.) pertaining to 2016. The base year for the study is 2016, with 2017 as forecast period.
