NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
CooperKatz & Company, Inc., an award winning and results-driven public relations firm currently celebrating its 20th anniversary, today announced three senior-level promotions.
“Each of these individuals shares a deep commitment to the development and success of our agency,” said Ralph Katz, co-founder and principal of CooperKatz. “In today’s highly-competitive marketplace, it is notable when talented, driven individuals choose to grow along with an organization. It is an honor for us to recognize the significant contributions they have already made to our business, as well as to empower them to do even more going forward.”
About CooperKatz & Company
CooperKatz is an award-winning, independent and results-driven public relations and communications firm with strong strategic, media relations, digital and creative services credentials. The agency offers a full-service public relations capability to a national client base across a broad range of industry sectors, including: business-to-business; consumer products / services; financial services; consumer finance; professional services; healthcare; technology; telecommunications; transportation; government; and non-profit organizations.
More information about CooperKatz can be found at www.cooperkatz.com.
