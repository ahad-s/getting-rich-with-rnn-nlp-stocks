WILMINGTON, Del.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Rigrodsky & Long, P.A.:
Rigrodsky & Long, P.A. announces that it is investigating potential legal claims against the board of directors of Universal American Corp. (“Universal American” or the “Company”) (NYSE: UAM) regarding possible breaches of fiduciary duties and other violations of law related to the Company’s entry into an agreement to be acquired by WellCare Health Plans, Inc. (“WellCare”) (NYSE: WCG), in a transaction valued at approximately $600 million.
Click here to learn more: http://rigrodskylong.com/investigations/universal-american-corp-uam.
Under the terms of the agreement, shareholders of Universal American Corp. will receive $10.00 in cash for each share of Universal American Corp. common stock.
The investigation concerns whether Universal American’s board of directors failed to adequately shop the Company and obtain the best possible value for Universal American shareholders before entering into an agreement with WellCare.
