NEW YORK--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
taxhub™ has been named a silver winner in best new service of the year in Best in Biz Awards, the only independent business awards program judged by members of the press and industry analysts.
This Smart News Release features multimedia. View the full release here: http://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20161209005156/en/ 
The sixth annual program in North America garnered more than 600 entries, from an impressive array of public and private companies from a variety of industries and geographic regions in the U.S. and Canada. Best in Biz Awards 2016 honors were presented in several categories, including Company of the Year, Fastest-Growing Company of the Year, Most Innovative Company of the Year, Most Innovative Product of the Year, Best New Product of the Year, App of the Year.
George Birrell, Co-Founder taxhub™
“Winning this award amongst the field of public and highly capitalized firms confirms the adage; Innovation: small businesses live it, big businesses buy it. At taxhub™, we believe it’s time to bring the transparency of the new economy to places that haven’t embraced it yet. And the tax business is one of them”
Winners of Best in Biz Awards 2016 were determined based on scoring from an independent panel of 50 judges from widely known newspapers, business, consumer and technology publications, TV outlets, and analyst firms. This year’s panel included several worthy additions to the high-profile group. The panel included Accounting Today, AdWeek, Associated Press, Business News Daily, CNET, Computerworld, Consumer Affairs, Entrepreneur, Forbes, Information Week, InfoWorld, Investment Advisor Magazine, Wall Street Journal, Wired and ZDNet.
About taxhub™
taxhub™ is a virtual, personal income tax preparation platform that recreates a face-to-face tax preparation experience through various desktop and smart-phone communication file sharing mediums. The company combines the convenience and low price of in-home online tax filing with the assurance of having a CPA prepared tax return. Their platform allows users to communicate and share income tax documents with tax professionals just as if the customer was sitting across the desk from them.
