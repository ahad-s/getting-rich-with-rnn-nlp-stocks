HOUSTON--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Swift Energy Company (SWTF) (the “Company”) announced today that it closed its previously announced divestment of the Lake Washington field in South East Louisiana.
The net proceeds received by Swift Energy ($40.0 million, less customary closing adjustments) will be used to reduce the amount of borrowings under the Company’s credit facility which was approximately $245 million prior to receipt of these funds.
About Swift Energy Company
Swift Energy Company, founded in 1979 and headquartered in Houston, engages in developing, exploring, acquiring and operating oil and gas properties, with a focus on the Eagle Ford trend of South Texas.
