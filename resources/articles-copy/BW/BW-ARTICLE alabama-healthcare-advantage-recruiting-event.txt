BIRMINGHAM, Ala.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
Alabama Healthcare Advantage, part of the Centene Fortune 200 family, today announces a two-day recruiting event from 12 – 8 PM on Wednesday, December 14th and Thursday, December 15th at the Hilton Birmingham Perimeter Park, 8 Perimeter Park S in Birmingham.
“We are looking to hire new senior leadership positions in managed care across Alabama,” says Scott Shelton, President at Shelton Consulting Services of Alabama. The Centene family is a Fortune 200 company with 35 years of financial stability and currently the fourth fastest growing company in the U.S.
The company will be hiring for directors, managers, trainers and more. The Alabama Healthcare Advantage jobs come with competitive salaries and benefits for top talent. The new positions will be located in Birmingham, Florence, Tuscaloosa, Montgomery, and Mobile.
“We are asking potential new hires to bring their resume with them to one of our two-day recruiting events, along with a passion to transform the healthcare of the community,” added Kara House, Vice President, Business Implementation & Health Plan Initiatives at Centene Corporation.
For more information:
www.alabamahealthadvantage.com/recruitingevent
About Centene Corporation: Centene Corporation, a Fortune 200 company, is a diversified, multi-national healthcare enterprise that provides a portfolio of services to government sponsored healthcare programs, focusing on under-insured and uninsured individuals.
