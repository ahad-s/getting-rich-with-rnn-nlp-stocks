MENLO PARK, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--
TriplePoint Venture Growth BDC Corp. (TPVG) (the “Company”), the leading financing provider to venture growth stage companies in the technology, life sciences and other high growth industries backed by a select group of leading venture capital investors, today announced that Jim Labe, chief executive officer and chairman of the board, and Sajal Srivastava, president and chief investment officer, will speak about the Company during the 2016 Wells Fargo Middle Market Opportunities Forum in New York City on Wednesday, December 7, 2016 at 10.00 a.m. Eastern Time and participate in one-on-one meetings.
About TriplePoint Venture Growth BDC Corp.
TriplePoint Venture Growth BDC Corp. (the “Company”) (TPVG) is an externally managed, closed-end, non-diversified management investment company that has elected to be regulated as a business development company under the Investment Company Act of 1940, as amended. It was formed to expand the venture growth stage business segment of its sponsor, TriplePoint Capital LLC. The Company’s investment objective is to maximize its total return to stockholders primarily in the form of current income and, to a lesser extent, capital appreciation by primarily lending with warrants to venture growth stage companies focused in technology, life sciences and other high growth industries backed by a select group of leading venture capital investors. More information is available at http://www.tpvg.com.
